💞 Couples Day · Friday, July 10

Day Trip

A romantic, adult-focused window between checkout and checking into the Westin — three relaxed ways to spend it, all within about an hour's drive.

The perfect in-between day 🌺

With checkout behind you and Westin check-in ahead, Friday is wide open for a couples-only adventure — no rushing, no crowds. Below are three distinct itineraries: a luxe upcountry garden + distillery tour, a private catamaran straight off Kaʻanapali, or a lush valley + jungle escape. Distances on the map are measured by car from the Westin, Kaʻanapali.

💖 Pro-tip for the in-between day: Pack a separate "day-use bag" with a change of nice clothes, walking shoes, and wallets, and keep it in the front of the trunk. Leave the heavy suitcases with the Westin's bell desk before you officially check in — so you're not driving the island with your whole life in the back of the car.

Option 1 · Modern Luxe Upcountry

Botanical garden + high-end distillery

Sleep in, then drive about an hour up into the crisp, cool slopes of Haleakalā for a gorgeous mid-morning.

10:00 AM⏱ ~1 hr from the Westin🕒 ~1–1.5 hrs here💵 ~$15/adult

Kula Botanical Garden

A breathtaking, multi-acre garden woven through rolling green hills, rock formations, waterfalls, and koi ponds. Massive ancient trees and unique tropical flora that feel a world away from the beach — quiet, peaceful, and perfect for walking hand-in-hand.

12:00 PM⏱ ~5 min from the garden🕒 ~1.5–2 hrs here💵 tasting ~$25/pp + lunch

Lunch & tasting at Ocean Vodka Organic Farm

A stunning 80-acre property just minutes away in Kula with sweeping, panoramic views of both Maui coastlines. Grab a gourmet, fresh lunch at the open-air café, walk through the organic sugar-cane fields, and do a spirit tasting — that farm-to-table vibe on a relaxed timeline.

Option 2 · Private "Doorstep" Charter

Private catamaran — no drive time

Skip the car entirely and use the fact that you're staying right on Kaʻanapali.

Midday · 3–4 hrs⏱ Walk to the beach — no drive💵 private ~$2–4k boat (≈$200/pp shared)

Private catamaran or yacht charter

Book a private catamaran or luxury motor yacht that departs right from the sand of Kaʻanapali — steps from where you're staying. A 3-to-4-hour private midday charter can take just the two of you across the channel to the dramatic sea cliffs of Lānaʻi or a tucked-away West Maui cove, with a captain and crew catering to you: high-end food, cocktails, and private snorkeling away from the crowds. You're back on the beach right in time for resort check-in.

⛵ Operators to check: Sea Maui, Gemini, or Kaʻanapali Ocean Adventures all run beach-departure charters.

Option 3 · Lush Valley & Deep Jungle

Towering green peaks + a tropical plantation lunch

About a 40-minute drive toward Central/West Maui for dramatic mountains and true jungle.

9:30 AM⏱ ~40 min from the Westin🕒 ~1–1.5 hrs here💵 $5/pp + $10 parking

ʻĪao Valley State Monument

Drive into the dramatic, emerald-green peaks of Central Maui. The towering, carpeted volcanic needle is iconic — a short, romantic stroll along a rushing river surrounded by massive rainforest plants.

🅿️ Heads up: You must book a parking/entry reservation online in advance for ʻĪao Valley.

Maui Nui Botanical Gardens

Nearby in Kahului, Maui Nui focuses specifically on native Hawaiian and Polynesian plants — a quiet, living collection you won't see anywhere else. A peaceful, shaded stroll before lunch.

Maui Tropical Plantation & The Mill House

Hop over to the neighboring Maui Tropical Plantation in Waikapu for a beautiful lunch at The Mill House — dining right on the edge of a lagoon, looking up at the West Maui Mountains. A gorgeous, relaxed finish before heading back to check in.
