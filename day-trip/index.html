💞 Couples Day · Friday, July 10

Day Trip

A romantic, adult-focused window between checkout and checking into the Westin — five relaxed ways to spend it, all within about an hour's drive.

The perfect in-between day 🌺

With checkout behind you and Westin check-in ahead, Friday is wide open for a couples-only adventure — no rushing, no crowds. Below are five ways to spend it, from a luxe upcountry garden tour to a catamaran straight off Kaʻanapali. Distances on the map are measured by car from the Westin, Kaʻanapali.

💖 Pro-tip for the in-between day: Pack a separate "day-use bag" with a change of nice clothes, walking shoes, and wallets, and keep it in the front of the trunk. Leave the heavy suitcases with the Westin's bell desk before you officially check in — so you're not driving the island with your whole life in the back of the car.

Botanical garden + high-end distillery

Option 2 · Snorkel & Sail Off Kaʻanapali

Catamaran from the beach — no drive time

Midday · 2.5–5 hrs⏱ Walk to the beach — no drive💵 ~$130–200/pp (shared)

Snorkel & sail catamaran

Option 3 · Lush Valley & Deep Jungle

Towering green peaks + a tropical plantation lunch

About a 40-minute drive toward Central/West Maui for dramatic mountains and true jungle.

9:30 AM⏱ ~40 min from the Westin🕒 ~1–1.5 hrs here💵 $5/pp + $10 parking

ʻĪao Valley State Monument

Drive into the dramatic, emerald-green peaks of Central Maui. The towering, carpeted volcanic needle is iconic — a short, romantic stroll along a rushing river surrounded by rainforest.

  • See the iconic 1,200-ft emerald "needle" up close
  • Short, shaded riverside walk — easy and romantic
  • Dramatic rainforest peaks minutes from Central Maui
🅿️ Heads up: You must book a parking/entry reservation online in advance for ʻĪao Valley.

Maui Nui Botanical Gardens

Nearby in Kahului, Maui Nui focuses specifically on native Hawaiian and Polynesian plants — a quiet, living collection you won't see anywhere else.

  • Rare native Hawaiian & Polynesian plants found nowhere else
  • Quiet and uncrowded — a peaceful pre-lunch wander
  • Real cultural & botanical story of the islands
